About this website

This website started as a joke to amuse my friends in the office and to have a really cool email address. Later, I decided to make it a blog to order my ideas. That was too boring, so in this next iteration is a mixture of a joke and ideas.

The design is unintentionally brutalist . I wanted to remove as much clutter as possible and let people read it in any browser they want (this works well on lynx ).

On the tech side, I'm using Pelican and my own theme .
