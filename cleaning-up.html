Cleaning Up

Jun 2022
By Ruben Serradas
Category: cleanup

I've been writing, sporadically, since the last three years.

It has been a good experience: I enjoy maintaining this little garden on the internet.

As a gardener, I have to get rid of the weed from time to time:

  • Articles in which I made plans I didn't want to continue.
  • Thoughtless comments on areas of knowledge I didn't know enough.
  • Words which simply don't represent me anymore.

Therefore, I'm just starting all over again. There's a lot of noise out there and I don't want to contribute to that.
